Question: How does the interactive 093 support number works?
Answer: Untitled document

This interactive voice response system provides a convenient way to access information on the services you have at GT&T.

To use this service:

  • Dial the toll free number 093
  • List for instructions
  • By Pressing 1 through 5 on the telephone pad you gain access to the type of information.

For example: 1: if you want to check your account balance, including all payments made to date.
