GT&T 10/10


GT&T 10/10 Soft Ball Cricket

Ten10 is a shortened, exciting version of competitive softball cricket developed by GT&T.

GT&T’s vision is to foster community involvement in Sports in a competitive manner while at the same time up keeping the art and skill of Soft Ball Cricket, part of Guyanese Culture.

 The game caters for everyone: young or old, male or female and from every street, village and community across Guyana.

Ten10 has become an  Annual event in Guyana that provide a platform to highlight the cricketing skills of Guyanese while promoting good leadership skills and discipline among teams. It is held in the first quarter every year at grounds across the country; from Berbice to Essequibo, Lethem to Mahdia.

GT&T also uses 10/10 as an avenue to give back to its customers. Thousands of GT&T’s customers are provided an opportunity during the 10/10 series to win millions of dollars’ worth in talk time and/or memorabilia of GT&T’s 10/10 bats, balls, hats, bags, signature bats among other great prizes.

The support has been enormous and GT&T is pleased to have introduced this competitive sport and create another legend in Guyanese cricketing sport.
