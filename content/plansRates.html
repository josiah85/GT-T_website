Post Paid Mobile Service

We rollover your unused minutes, regardless of your plan. All unused "free" minutes in any month are carried over to the next 2 months. So, if your Plan has 50 free minutes and you utilize 30, then the residual 20 minutes are rolled over to the following month and you start with 70 free minutes. Always, you must use your rollover minutes first each month and then your monthly free minutes.
