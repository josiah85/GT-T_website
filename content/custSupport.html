Customer Support


At GT&T our customers are our biggest asset. While quality of service is our number one priority, customer care and support is most important to us.

We have expanded our support services to allow you to easily access our support team. You can contact our call centre or you can make a reoprt using our new online fault reporting service.

For all GT&T products and services call our toll free number 0488. For specific assistance, dial 0488 and:

  • Press 1 for DSL/Emagine and Internet assistance (Check on status of applications, get billing updates, make payment enquiries, obtain information on related products and services, etc.)
  • Press 2 for wire line and mobile assistance (get billing updates, make payment enquiries, obtain information on handsets and promotions, request features, etc.)
  • Press 3 for electronic (C-Point) and vendor issues (pre-paid card enquiries, C-Point credit adjustments, etc.)
  • Press 4 for fault repairs (report on line faults, etc,)

Dial 092 for Directory Assistance.
