Data Plans for Pre-Paid BlackBerry Subscribers

If as a pre-paid mobile customer you have a BlackBerry handheld and wish to subscribe to GT&T’s BlackBerry service, simply text BB1D (for a 1-day subscription), BB1W (for a 1-week subscription), or BB1M (for a 1-month subscription) to 620-4227. The system will send you a confirmation message to ensure that you are expressing a serious interest. Once you acknowledge, and your account has a balance sufficient to cover the cost of the plan you are requesting, your handheld will be made ready.
Pre-paid BlackBerry customers have the option of subscribing to daily, weekly or monthly data plans as shown below.
