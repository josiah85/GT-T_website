Mobile Value Added Services

Some convenient features we offer to Enhance your GT&T Mobile Experience.

Voicemail

Receive messages when your phone is of or engaged or when you are not inclined to answer. Play back your messages at your convenience. We off this feature free of charge. The retrieval of messages costs $10 per minute plus VAT.

Caller ID

ID the phone number of the calling party when your mobile rings. We offer this feature free of charge. To suppress the feature attracts a one-off charge of $284.

Call Waiting

Use your mobile as though it has two separate lines and switch as often as you wish between a call you are on and a call that is coming in. . Each of your conversations remains private. We offer this feature free of charge.

Call Forwarding

Redirects your incoming calls to a pre-selected alternative phone number for the modest price of $213 per month.

Conference Calling

Enjoy the convenience of being able to add a third party to an ongoing call for as little as $320 per month. Of course, airtime charges will apply to both calls.
