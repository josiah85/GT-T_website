Question: How to STOP sms notifications from GT&T.
Answer: Untitled document

GT&T mobile subscribers can stop SMS notifications from terminating on their phones by sending a text message to 640-9997

The steps to unsubscribe from text advertisments are as follows;

  • type the words "STOP" in the sms message
  • send sms to '640-9997'

Once this is completed the subscriber will no longer receive SMS notifications from the company.
Question: How can I check my Pre-Paid Balance?
Answer: Untitled document

Dial *100# to send a network request for the balance on your pe-paid account. This service is free.
