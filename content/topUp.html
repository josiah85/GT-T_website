Mobile Top Up

Keep your mobile account topped up!
Whether you opt for our scratch cards or electronic top up, we make topping up easy. Our scratch cards and C-Point electronic top are available nationwide. Look for the C-Point sign.


Scratch Cards

These are plastic cards with a 13-digit PIN printed and concealed on the reverse side. The PIN is revealed by scratching the material that conceals it and then used to recharge (top up) your mobile account to the value of the card you purchased.
Your top up transaction will be confirmed by SMS.

Our scratch cards are available in denominations of $200, $500 and $1,000.


Ezetop

The EZETOP facility is an online service that allows you to instantly recharge the accounts of family and friends from anywhere in the world. We guarantee that it is simple, swift and secure. Click here to visit www.ezetop.com


Electronic C-Point Top Up

An alternative mobile top up facility is our electronic (C-Point) top up. This option allows you to give a vendor your mobile number and your top up amount and have the vendor use GT&T's electronic interface (on their mobile phone, PC or Dial-up Terminal) to credit your account.
Your top up transactions are confirmed by SMS. The top up amount can be as little as $100 and as much as $30,000, in three separate transactions.
