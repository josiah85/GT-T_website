Question: How does Emagine Broadband work?
Answer: Untitled document

Emagine Broadband uses your existing phone line hence there is no need to install an additional line. You can utilize your internet access while you are using your phone. This is done by splitting the phone line into two frequency ranges, namely low and high frequencies. Low frequencies are reserved for regular telephone calls (voice), while higher frequencies are used for Internet (data). Consequently, it enables the use of the phone line for both telephone calls and for Internet access.
